7. Add service area: Mississauga, Toronto, Brampton, Oakville, Etobicoke
8. Phone: **647-914-4176**
9. Website: **https://drivewayautorepair.ca**
10. **Verify** (Google mails a postcard with a code — takes 5-14 days, crucial step)
11. Once verified:
    - Upload 10-20 photos (shop exterior, interior, team, before/after work)
    - Set hours (Mon-Fri 8AM-7PM, Sat 9AM-5PM)
    - Add business description (use keywords: "auto body shop Mississauga", "collision repair", "insurance claims")
    - Post 1-2 updates per week (new projects, promotions)

**Once verified + photos added, you'll start appearing in Google Maps results within days.**

## 🎯 Priority 2: Google Search Console (5 min, FREE)

This tells Google to index your website and shows you what keywords you rank for.

### Steps:

1. Go to **https://search.google.com/search-console**
2. Sign in with `drivewayautoinc@gmail.com`
3. Click **Add Property** → enter: `https://drivewayautorepair.ca`
4. Choose **URL prefix** method
5. Verify via **HTML tag** (Google gives you a tag to add):
   - Copy the tag they provide (looks like: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="XXX" />`)
   - Paste it inside the `<head>` section of `index.html` on GitHub (just below the other meta tags)
   - Commit → Netlify redeploys → click Verify in Google Search Console
6. Once verified:
   - Go to **Sitemaps** → submit: `https://drivewayautorepair.ca/sitemap.xml`
   - Go to **URL Inspection** → enter your homepage URL → click **Request Indexing**

**Your site will start appearing in Google search within 1-7 days.**
