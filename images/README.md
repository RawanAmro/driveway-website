## Tips for great before/after pairs

- **Same angle, same distance, same lighting.** That's what makes the slider look dramatic.
- **JPG format**, 1200x800px or larger.
- **Under 500KB each** — compress at https://tinypng.com first.
- Crop them the same way (identical aspect ratio).

## Don't have all 6 cars?

No problem. Even 2 or 3 matched pairs look great. Empty slots show a clear "Drop [filename]" placeholder so you know what's missing.

## Want different car names?

Just tell me which 6 cars you want featured (make/model), and I'll rename the slots to match. Then your photos can be named accordingly.

## How to get photos into this folder

1. Open your Cowork workspace on your computer (same app you're using to talk to me).
2. Go to `outputs` → `driveway-website` → `images`.
3. Save your photos directly into that folder with the exact names above.
4. Re-zip `driveway-website` and drag onto netlify.com/drop again — your preview updates.
